Apples and Peaches
Fukushima Prefecture is famous for its fruit production, and especially for its peaches and apples, which boast some of the highest production volumes in the country. By the way, in creating the printing draft for a certain sales brochure, the descriptions for apples and peaches were mistakenly switched.
You have been tasked with the job of correcting "apple" and "peach." It's quite a hassle, but please create a program that takes in a line of English text (including alphanumeric characters, spaces, and symbols) and outputs a version of that text in which all occurrences of "apple" have been replaced with "peach," and all occurrences of "peach" have been replaced with "apple."
Input
A line of English text (including alphanumeric characters, spaces, and symbols) with a length of 1000 characters or less is given.
Output
Output a line of English text in which "apple" and "peach" have been swapped.
Sample Input
the cost of one peach is higher than that of one apple.
Output for the Sample Input
the cost of one apple is higher than that of one peach.
